Name: The Sky Project

Developer: Enthariel Games

Period: Nov 2024 - Sep 2025

Synopsis: In a world divided into four realms, where mana flows like rivers of power between worlds, a story of love, loss, and discovery unfolds. A celestial being from an apparently perfect world embarks on a journey that will challenge everything they know.
